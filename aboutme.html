About Kitty

Hi! I'm Kitty, a fantasy writer from the UK. I've been making up stories since I was 6, and started writing them down a few years ago. I have two pets, a 3 legged cat called Lucky and a no legged rosy boa called Ed. Don't worry - both of them are kept safely away from each other!

Besides writing, I enjoy spending time gaming, reading and watching Netflix. Some of my favourite games are Skyrim, Stray, Hollow Knight and Portal 2. I'm currently rewatching The Dragon Prince on Netflix, catching up ready for season 7 to drop later this year.
